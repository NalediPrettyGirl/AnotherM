Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to list an item?

It costs R49 per item — that is the only fee you will ever pay to Treasured. There are no subscriptions, no monthly fees, and no renewal charges.

Is this a once-off payment?

Yes. You pay R49 once when you publish your listing. Your item stays live for 12 months with no further charges required.

Does Treasured take commission when my item sells?

No. Treasured is a listing platform only. We earn the R49 listing fee and nothing else. We are not involved in the sale, so no commission is ever charged.

Can I list more than one item?

Yes. Each item requires its own R49 listing fee. Simply create a new listing for each piece of attire you want to sell.

What happens after my item sells?

Once your item is sold, mark it as sold in your account and the listing will be archived. No further fees apply and no renewal is needed.

What can I sell on Treasured?

Treasured is for formal attire only — wedding dresses and suits, evening and cocktail wear, traditional and cultural attire, matric dance outfits, bridal party wear, and formal accessories.

Does Treasured handle payment or delivery for my sale?

No. Treasured is purely a listing platform. All payment, collection, and delivery arrangements are made directly between you and your buyer. We are not involved in any transaction beyond the listing fee.

Platform disclaimer

Treasured is a listing platform only. We are not involved in payments between buyers and sellers, deliveries, fittings, or the handling of any items. The R49 listing fee is the only transaction between you and Treasured. All other arrangements are made directly between the buyer and seller.
